About CodingLotus

CodingLotus is a Junior Web Developer living in Montana. A Montana Code School Graduate, she is versed in Javascript, HTML, CSS, NodeJS, NoSQL databases (primarily MongoDB). Post graduation, she was then selected to continue the vision of the Montana Code School in the inaugural Bozeman, MT cohort of Summer 2016. She continues freelance work building sites with varying frameworks including ReactJS and Angular.

Born and raised under Big Sky Country, Lauren can be found frolicking among the forest or splashing about the many rivers throughout western Montana. Finding herself thriving in Missoula, MT, Lauren started her programming career in early 2016. Attending the Montana Code School provided fundamental skills for basic comprehension of computer programming. With a bright future ahead Lauren continues to learn, and pushes her boundaries to be the best in her field. Stay tuned.
